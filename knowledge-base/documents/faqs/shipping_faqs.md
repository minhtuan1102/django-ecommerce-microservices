# Frequently Asked Questions - Shipping & Delivery

## Shipping Options

### What shipping options are available?
We offer several shipping options:

| Option | Delivery Time | Cost |
|--------|---------------|------|
| Standard Shipping | 3-5 business days | 20,000 - 35,000 VND |
| Express Shipping | 1-2 business days | 45,000 - 60,000 VND |
| Same-Day Delivery | Same day (order before 2PM) | 70,000 VND |
| Free Shipping | 5-7 business days | Free (orders over 300,000 VND) |

### Do you offer free shipping?
Yes! Free shipping is available for:
- Orders over 300,000 VND
- Members with Premium membership
- Promotional periods (check our homepage for current offers)

### Do you ship internationally?
Currently, we ship within Vietnam only. We're working on expanding international shipping options.

### Can I ship to a PO Box?
No, we cannot ship to PO Boxes. Please provide a physical address for delivery.

## Delivery Information

### How long does delivery take?
Delivery times depend on your location and shipping method:
- **Ho Chi Minh City & Hanoi**: 1-3 business days
- **Other major cities**: 2-4 business days
- **Rural areas**: 4-7 business days

### What happens if I'm not home during delivery?
If you're not available:
1. The courier will attempt to contact you
2. A second delivery attempt will be made the next business day
3. After 3 failed attempts, the package returns to our warehouse
4. You can reschedule delivery or pick up from the courier's location

### Can I schedule a specific delivery time?
For Express and Same-Day shipping, you can select a preferred 2-hour delivery window during checkout (availability varies by area).

### Do I need to sign for my package?
- Orders under 500,000 VND: No signature required
- Orders 500,000 VND and above: Signature required
- All COD orders: Signature required

## Tracking & Updates

### How do I track my order?
1. Check your email for shipping confirmation with tracking number
2. Visit our website and go to "Track Order"
3. Enter your order number and email
4. View real-time tracking updates

### Why isn't my tracking information updating?
Tracking updates may be delayed due to:
- Courier system updates (usually within 24 hours)
- Package in transit between facilities
- Weekend or holiday processing

### Will I receive shipping notifications?
Yes! You'll receive notifications at these stages:
- Order confirmed
- Order shipped (with tracking number)
- Out for delivery
- Delivered

### My tracking shows "Delivered" but I didn't receive my package
Please check:
1. With neighbors or building reception
2. Around your delivery location (doorstep, mailroom)
3. With household members

If still not found, contact us within 48 hours with your order number.

## Address & Delivery Issues

### How do I change my delivery address?
You can change your address before the order ships:
1. Go to "My Orders"
2. Select the order
3. Click "Change Address" if available
4. Or contact customer support immediately

### Can I redirect my package to a different address?
Once shipped, address changes incur a redirect fee (15,000 VND). Contact support with your tracking number and new address.

### What if my address was entered incorrectly?
Contact us immediately. If the package hasn't shipped, we can correct it. If already shipped, we'll coordinate with the courier for re-routing.

### Do you deliver to office addresses?
Yes! When entering an office address, please include:
- Company name
- Floor/suite number
- Your name and phone number
- Office hours for delivery

## Special Deliveries

### Can I request gift wrapping?
Yes! Gift wrapping is available for 25,000 VND per item. Select this option during checkout and add a personalized message.

### Do you offer gift receipts?
Yes, select "This is a gift" during checkout. A gift receipt without prices will be included.

### Can I combine multiple orders into one shipment?
If orders are placed within 24 hours and haven't been processed, contact support to combine them (saves on shipping!).

### How do pre-orders ship?
Pre-ordered items ship when they become available. If your order contains both in-stock and pre-order items:
- Default: Ship together when all items available
- Option: Ship in-stock items first (additional shipping may apply)

## Shipping Problems

### My package is damaged. What should I do?
1. Take photos of the damaged packaging AND contents
2. Contact customer support within 48 hours
3. We'll arrange a replacement or refund

### My package shows delivered but is missing items
Check your order confirmation to verify items ordered. Contact support with:
- Order number
- List of missing items
- Photos of packaging if unsealed

### What if delivery is significantly delayed?
If your package is delayed beyond estimated delivery:
1. Check tracking for updates
2. Contact the courier directly
3. If no resolution, contact our support for a refund or re-shipment

### Can I refuse a delivery?
Yes, you can refuse a delivery. The package will be returned to us, and you'll receive a refund (minus original shipping costs, unless it was our error).

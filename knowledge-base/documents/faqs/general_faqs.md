# Frequently Asked Questions - General

## Account & Registration

### How do I create an account?
To create an account:
1. Click "Sign Up" or "Register" on the homepage
2. Enter your email address and create a password
3. Fill in your personal information (name, phone number)
4. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox
5. Start shopping!

### I forgot my password. How can I reset it?
To reset your password:
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email for a password reset link
5. Click the link and create a new password

### How do I update my account information?
Log into your account, go to "My Account" or "Profile Settings", and you can update:
- Personal information (name, phone)
- Email address
- Password
- Shipping addresses
- Payment methods

### Can I have multiple shipping addresses?
Yes! You can save multiple shipping addresses in your account. During checkout, simply select the address you want to use or add a new one.

### How do I delete my account?
To delete your account, please contact our customer support team at support@bookstore.com. Note that deleting your account will remove all your order history and saved information.

## Orders & Purchasing

### How do I place an order?
1. Browse or search for products
2. Add items to your cart
3. Click "Checkout"
4. Enter or select shipping address
5. Choose payment method
6. Review and confirm your order

### Can I modify my order after placing it?
Orders can be modified within 30 minutes of placement if they haven't been processed yet. Contact customer support immediately if you need to make changes.

### How do I cancel an order?
You can cancel an order from "My Orders" page if it hasn't been shipped yet. Once shipped, you'll need to wait for delivery and initiate a return instead.

### How do I track my order?
After your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also:
1. Log into your account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Click on the order to see tracking details

### Why was my order cancelled?
Orders may be cancelled if:
- Item is out of stock
- Payment failed or was declined
- Suspected fraudulent activity
- Unable to verify shipping address

## Payment

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept:
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, JCB)
- Bank Transfer
- E-wallets (MoMo, ZaloPay, VNPay)
- Cash on Delivery (COD)

### Is my payment information secure?
Yes! We use industry-standard SSL encryption and never store your full card details on our servers. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways.

### When will my card be charged?
Your card is charged when your order is confirmed. For pre-orders, you'll be charged when the item ships.

### My payment was declined. What should I do?
1. Verify your card details are correct
2. Check if your card has sufficient funds
3. Contact your bank to ensure the transaction isn't blocked
4. Try a different payment method

## Products & Availability

### How do I search for a specific book?
Use the search bar at the top of the page. You can search by:
- Book title
- Author name
- ISBN
- Keywords

### Are all products on your website in stock?
Product pages show real-time availability. Items marked "In Stock" are available for immediate shipping. "Pre-order" items will ship when available.

### Can I be notified when an out-of-stock item becomes available?
Yes! Click "Notify Me" on the product page to receive an email when it's back in stock.

### Do you sell e-books?
Yes, we offer e-books for many titles. Look for the "E-book" format option on product pages. E-books are delivered instantly to your account after purchase.

### Can I return a digital purchase?
Digital purchases (e-books, audiobooks) are generally non-refundable due to their nature. However, if you experience technical issues, please contact support.

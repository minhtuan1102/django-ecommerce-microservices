# Return & Refund Policy

*Last Updated: April 2026*

## Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This policy explains how to return items and receive refunds.

## Return Eligibility

### Returnable Items
You may return most items within **30 days** of delivery if:
- Item is in original, unused condition
- Original packaging is intact
- All accessories/materials included
- Proof of purchase provided

### Non-Returnable Items
The following cannot be returned:
- **E-books and digital downloads** (delivered electronically)
- **Audiobooks** (digital format)
- **Gift cards**
- **Magazines and periodicals**
- **Items marked "Final Sale"**
- **Personalized or custom items**
- **Items damaged by customer**

### Damaged or Defective Items
Items arriving damaged or defective can be returned regardless of the above restrictions. Please report within **48 hours** of delivery.

## Return Process

### Step 1: Initiate Return
1. Log into your account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Select the order containing the item
4. Click "Return Item"
5. Select reason for return
6. Choose refund method

### Step 2: Prepare Package
- Pack items securely in original packaging
- Include all original materials
- Print and attach return label
- Note your return authorization number

### Step 3: Ship Return
- Drop off at designated courier location
- Or schedule a pickup (fee may apply)
- Keep your shipping receipt

### Step 4: Refund Processing
- We inspect returned items within 3 business days
- Approved refunds processed within 5 business days
- Refund appears in 5-10 business days (varies by bank)

## Return Shipping

### Free Returns
Return shipping is free when:
- Item is damaged or defective
- Wrong item was shipped
- Item doesn't match description

### Customer-Paid Returns
You pay return shipping when:
- Changed your mind
- Item didn't meet expectations (not defective)
- Ordered wrong item

### Return Shipping Cost
- Standard return: 25,000 VND (deducted from refund)
- Express pickup: 45,000 VND

## Refund Options

### Original Payment Method
Refunds to the original payment method:
- Credit/Debit Card: 5-10 business days
- Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days
- E-wallet: 1-3 business days

### Store Credit
- Processed immediately
- Valid for 1 year
- Can be used on any purchase
- Bonus: 5% extra credit on refund value

### Exchange
- Exchange for same item (different edition)
- Exchange for item of equal or lesser value
- Pay difference for higher-value item

## Refund Amounts

### Full Refund
You receive a full refund when:
- Item is defective or damaged
- Wrong item shipped
- Item significantly not as described
- Order cancelled before shipping

### Partial Refund
Deductions may apply for:
- Missing original packaging: Up to 20% deduction
- Signs of use: Up to 50% deduction
- Missing accessories: Cost of missing items deducted
- Customer-paid return shipping

### Non-Refundable Costs
- Original shipping costs (unless our error)
- Gift wrapping fees
- COD fees

## Condition Requirements

### "Like New" Condition
To receive full refund, items must be:
- Unread or minimally opened
- No writing, highlighting, or marks
- No creases, tears, or damage
- Original shrink wrap preferred

### "Good" Condition
Partial refund (up to 80%) for:
- Minor signs of handling
- Original packaging opened
- No damage to the item itself

### "Fair" Condition
Partial refund (up to 50%) for:
- Obvious signs of use
- Minor marks or creases
- May not be resellable as new

### "Poor" Condition
Return may be rejected for:
- Significant damage
- Writing or highlighting
- Missing pages
- Water damage

## Special Situations

### Gifts
If you received an item as a gift:
- Return for store credit or exchange
- Gift receipt required (if provided)
- Refund goes to gift recipient as store credit

### Promotional Items
Items purchased with discounts:
- Refund amount = price actually paid
- Buy-one-get-one: Both items must be returned
- Free items with purchase: Must return with qualifying item

### Bundles and Sets
- Complete set must be returned for full refund
- Individual items from a set may be exchanged only

### Pre-Orders
- Cancel anytime before shipping for full refund
- After shipping, standard return policy applies

## Processing Timeline

| Stage | Timeframe |
|-------|-----------|
| Return request approval | 1 business day |
| Return shipping (customer) | 5-7 business days |
| Item inspection | 3 business days |
| Refund processing | 5 business days |
| Funds in account | 5-10 business days |

**Total typical time**: 15-25 business days

## Exceptions

### Extended Returns
We offer extended return periods:
- Holiday purchases (Nov 15 - Dec 31): Return until Jan 31
- Premium members: 60-day return window

### Damaged in Transit
If the return package is damaged in transit:
- Keep all packaging materials
- Document damage with photos
- Contact us immediately
- We'll file claim and process refund

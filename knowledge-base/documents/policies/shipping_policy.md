# Shipping Policy

*Last Updated: April 2026*

## Overview

This Shipping Policy outlines how we handle the delivery of products purchased from our bookstore. We strive to deliver your orders accurately and on time.

## Shipping Coverage

### Domestic Shipping
We currently ship to all provinces and cities within Vietnam. Delivery times and costs vary by location.

### International Shipping
International shipping is not available at this time. We are working to expand our delivery network.

## Shipping Methods & Costs

### Standard Shipping
- **Delivery Time**: 3-5 business days
- **Cost**: 20,000 - 35,000 VND (based on weight and location)
- **Tracking**: Available

### Express Shipping
- **Delivery Time**: 1-2 business days
- **Cost**: 45,000 - 60,000 VND
- **Tracking**: Available with real-time updates

### Same-Day Delivery
- **Availability**: Ho Chi Minh City and Hanoi only
- **Order Cutoff**: 2:00 PM local time
- **Cost**: 70,000 VND
- **Tracking**: Available with live driver tracking

### Free Shipping
Free standard shipping is available for:
- Orders totaling 300,000 VND or more
- Premium membership holders (all orders)
- Special promotional periods

## Shipping Rates by Weight

| Weight | Standard | Express |
|--------|----------|---------|
| Up to 500g | 20,000 VND | 45,000 VND |
| 500g - 1kg | 25,000 VND | 50,000 VND |
| 1kg - 2kg | 30,000 VND | 55,000 VND |

| Over 5kg | Contact support | Contact support |

## Order Processing

### Processing Time
- Orders placed before 2:00 PM (Mon-Fri): Processed same day
- Orders placed after 2:00 PM: Processed next business day
- Weekend orders: Processed Monday

### Business Days
Monday through Friday, excluding Vietnamese public holidays.

### Order Confirmation
You will receive an email confirmation within 30 minutes of placing your order, including:
- Order number
- Items ordered
- Estimated delivery date
- Shipping address

## Delivery Information

### Delivery Attempts
- Up to 3 delivery attempts will be made
- Attempts are made on consecutive business days
- After 3 failed attempts, the package is returned to our warehouse

### Delivery Signatures
Signature is required for:
- Orders valued at 500,000 VND or more
- Cash on Delivery (COD) orders
- Specified high-value items

### Contactless Delivery
Upon request, we offer contactless delivery:
- Package left at designated location
- Photo confirmation sent to customer
- Not available for COD or signature-required orders

## Address Requirements

Please ensure your shipping address includes:
- Full name (as registered)
- Complete street address
- Ward/District
- City/Province
- Phone number (for delivery contact)

### Address Verification
We may contact you to verify:
- New shipping addresses
- Addresses flagged by our system
- Orders over 2,000,000 VND

## Shipping Restrictions

### Cannot Ship
- To PO Boxes
- To locations outside Vietnam
- Hazardous materials (not applicable to books)

### Remote Areas
Some remote locations may:
- Require additional 2-3 business days
- Incur additional shipping charges
- Have limited delivery options

## Lost or Damaged Shipments

### Damaged Packages
If your package arrives damaged:
1. Document damage with photos
2. Note damage on delivery receipt if signing
3. Contact us within 48 hours
4. We will arrange replacement or refund

### Lost Packages
If tracking shows delivered but not received:
1. Check with neighbors/building staff
2. Contact us within 7 days
3. We will investigate with the courier
4. Resolution provided within 5 business days

### Compensation
- Full replacement for damaged items (our fault)
- Full refund if replacement not available
- Shipping costs refunded for damaged deliveries

## Cash on Delivery (COD)

### Availability
COD is available for orders up to 5,000,000 VND.

### Payment
- Exact cash or close to order amount
- Some couriers accept card payment
- Receipt provided upon payment

### COD Fee
A COD handling fee of 15,000 VND applies.

## Changes to Shipments

### Before Shipping
- Address changes: Free
- Cancel order: Full refund

### After Shipping
- Address redirect: 15,000 VND fee
- Return to sender: Original shipping not refunded

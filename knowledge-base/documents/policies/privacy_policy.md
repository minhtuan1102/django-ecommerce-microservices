# Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: April 2026*

## Introduction

At Bookstore ("we", "our", "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you create an account or make a purchase, we collect:
- **Identity Data**: Full name, username
- **Contact Data**: Email address, phone number, shipping/billing address
- **Financial Data**: Payment card details (processed by secure payment providers)
- **Transaction Data**: Purchase history, order details
- **Profile Data**: Preferences, interests, feedback, reviews

### Automatically Collected Information
When you visit our website, we automatically collect:
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, search queries
- **Location Data**: General geographic location based on IP
- **Cookies**: Session and preference data (see Cookie Policy below)

### Information from Third Parties
We may receive information from:
- Payment processors (transaction confirmations)
- Social media platforms (if you connect accounts)
- Analytics providers (aggregated usage statistics)

## How We Use Your Information

### Provide Services
- Process and fulfill orders
- Manage your account
- Provide customer support
- Send order confirmations and updates

### Improve Experience
- Personalize product recommendations
- Analyze usage to improve our website
- Develop new features and services

### Marketing (With Consent)
- Send promotional emails and offers
- Display targeted advertisements
- Notify about new products and sales

### Legal Obligations
- Comply with laws and regulations
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Respond to legal requests

## Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:
- **Contract Performance**: Necessary to fulfill our agreement with you
- **Legitimate Interests**: Improve services, prevent fraud
- **Consent**: Marketing communications, cookies
- **Legal Obligation**: Tax records, fraud prevention

## How We Share Your Information

### Service Providers
We share data with trusted partners who help us operate:
- Payment processors (transaction processing)
- Shipping carriers (delivery)
- Cloud hosting providers (data storage)
- Analytics services (usage analysis)
- Customer support tools (help desk)

### Business Purposes
We may share data:
- Within our company for legitimate business purposes
- With legal authorities when required by law
- In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
- To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

### We Never Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

## Data Retention

We retain your information:
- **Account Data**: Until you delete your account + 30 days
- **Transaction Records**: 5 years (legal requirement)
- **Marketing Preferences**: Until you unsubscribe
- **Cookies**: See Cookie Policy for duration

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

### Access
Request a copy of your personal data we hold.

### Correction
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

### Deletion
Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal requirements).

### Portability
Receive your data in a portable format.

### Object
Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

### Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent for processing based on consent (e.g., marketing).

### Restriction
Request limitation of processing in certain circumstances.

### Complain
Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@bookstore.com.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:
- SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
- Encrypted storage of sensitive data
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits
- Employee training on data protection
- Secure payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Cookie Policy

### What Are Cookies
Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide and improve our services.

### Types of Cookies We Use

| Type | Purpose | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| Essential | Site functionality, security | Session |
| Functional | Preferences, language | 1 year |
| Analytics | Usage statistics | 2 years |
| Marketing | Targeted advertising | 90 days |

### Managing Cookies
You can control cookies through:
- Browser settings (block or delete cookies)
- Our cookie preference center
- Opt-out tools for specific providers

Note: Blocking essential cookies may affect site functionality.

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

Parents/guardians who believe their child has provided us information should contact us at privacy@bookstore.com.

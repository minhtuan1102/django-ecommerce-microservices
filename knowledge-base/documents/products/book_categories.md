# Book Categories Guide

## Fiction

### Literary Fiction
Thought-provoking narratives that explore the human condition. Often character-driven with emphasis on prose quality and themes over plot.

**Popular Authors**: Nguyễn Nhật Ánh, Haruki Murakami, Gabriel García Márquez
**Bestsellers**: Norwegian Wood, One Hundred Years of Solitude, Cho Tôi Xin Một Vé Đi Tuổi Thơ

### Mystery & Thriller
Stories centered on crime, suspense, and investigation. Keeps readers guessing until the final pages.

**Sub-genres**: Detective fiction, Psychological thriller, Legal thriller, Crime fiction
**Popular Authors**: Agatha Christie, Dan Brown, Lee Child
**Bestsellers**: The Da Vinci Code, Gone Girl, The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo

### Science Fiction
Imaginative fiction based on scientific concepts, futuristic technology, space exploration, and alternative realities.

**Sub-genres**: Hard SF, Space Opera, Cyberpunk, Dystopian, Time Travel
**Popular Authors**: Isaac Asimov, Philip K. Dick, Liu Cixin
**Bestsellers**: The Three-Body Problem, Dune, 1984

### Fantasy
Stories set in imaginary worlds with magical elements, mythical creatures, and supernatural phenomena.

**Sub-genres**: Epic Fantasy, Urban Fantasy, Dark Fantasy, Sword & Sorcery
**Popular Authors**: J.R.R. Tolkien, J.K. Rowling, Brandon Sanderson
**Bestsellers**: Harry Potter series, The Lord of the Rings, The Name of the Wind

### Romance
Stories centered on romantic relationships with emotionally satisfying endings.

**Sub-genres**: Contemporary, Historical, Paranormal, Romantic Suspense
**Popular Authors**: Nicholas Sparks, Nora Roberts, Colleen Hoover
**Bestsellers**: The Notebook, It Ends with Us, Beach Read

### Horror
Fiction designed to frighten, scare, or disgust readers through supernatural or psychological means.

**Popular Authors**: Stephen King, Dean Koontz, Paul Tremblay
**Bestsellers**: It, The Shining, A Head Full of Ghosts

## Non-Fiction

### Self-Help & Personal Development
Books aimed at self-improvement in various aspects of life including productivity, relationships, and mindset.

**Topics**: Habits, Productivity, Motivation, Mindfulness, Success
**Popular Authors**: James Clear, Dale Carnegie, Brené Brown
**Bestsellers**: Atomic Habits, How to Win Friends and Influence People, Đắc Nhân Tâm

### Business & Economics
Books on entrepreneurship, management, investing, and economic principles.

**Topics**: Leadership, Marketing, Finance, Entrepreneurship, Strategy
**Popular Authors**: Robert Kiyosaki, Peter Thiel, Simon Sinek
**Bestsellers**: Rich Dad Poor Dad, Zero to One, Start with Why

### Biography & Memoir
True stories of people's lives written by themselves (memoir) or others (biography).

**Types**: Autobiographies, Celebrity memoirs, Political biographies, Historical figures
**Popular Examples**: Steve Jobs by Walter Isaacson, Becoming by Michelle Obama, Educated by Tara Westover

### History
Non-fiction accounts of past events, civilizations, wars, and historical figures.

**Areas**: World History, Vietnam History, Military History, Social History
**Popular Authors**: Yuval Noah Harari, David McCullough
**Bestsellers**: Sapiens, The Rise and Fall of the Third Reich

### Science & Nature
Books explaining scientific concepts, discoveries, and the natural world for general audiences.

**Topics**: Physics, Biology, Environment, Astronomy, Mathematics
**Popular Authors**: Stephen Hawking, Carl Sagan, Richard Dawkins
**Bestsellers**: A Brief History of Time, Cosmos, The Selfish Gene

### Health & Fitness
Guides on physical and mental health, nutrition, exercise, and wellness.

**Topics**: Nutrition, Exercise, Mental Health, Medicine, Diet
**Popular Examples**: Why We Sleep, The Body Keeps the Score, Atomic Habits

## Academic & Professional

### Textbooks
Educational materials for students at various levels from primary to university.

**Subjects**: Mathematics, Sciences, Languages, Social Studies, Computer Science

### Reference
Books for quick consultation rather than continuous reading.

**Types**: Dictionaries, Encyclopedias, Almanacs, Atlases, Manuals

### Professional & Technical
Specialized books for professionals in specific fields.

**Fields**: Law, Medicine, Engineering, IT, Finance, Architecture

## Children & Young Adult

### Picture Books
Illustrated books for young children (ages 0-8) with minimal text.

### Middle Grade
Books for readers ages 8-12, often featuring young protagonists in adventures.

**Popular Series**: Diary of a Wimpy Kid, Percy Jackson, Harry Potter (early books)

### Young Adult (YA)
Books for teenagers (ages 12-18) dealing with coming-of-age themes.

**Popular Genres**: Contemporary, Fantasy, Dystopian, Romance
**Bestsellers**: The Hunger Games, The Fault in Our Stars, Six of Crows

## Vietnamese Literature

### Classic Vietnamese Literature
Traditional and modern classic works of Vietnamese literature.

**Notable Works**: Truyện Kiều, Số Đỏ, Chí Phèo, Tắt Đèn
**Notable Authors**: Nguyễn Du, Vũ Trọng Phụng, Nam Cao

### Contemporary Vietnamese Fiction
Modern Vietnamese novels and short stories.

**Popular Authors**: Nguyễn Nhật Ánh, Nguyễn Ngọc Tư, Dương Thụy
**Bestsellers**: Mắt Biếc, Cánh Đồng Bất Tận, Tôi Thấy Hoa Vàng Trên Cỏ Xanh

## Format Guide

### Hardcover
- Durable, high-quality binding
- Premium feel, makes great gifts
- Higher price point
- Best for: Collectors, gift-giving, reference books

### Paperback
- Lighter and more portable
- More affordable
- Less durable than hardcover
- Best for: Casual reading, travel, budget-conscious readers

### E-book
- Instant delivery
- No physical storage needed
- Often cheapest option
- Best for: Frequent travelers, space-conscious readers, immediate access

### Audiobook
- Listen while doing other activities
- Professional narration
- Available for download or streaming
- Best for: Commuters, multitaskers, auditory learners

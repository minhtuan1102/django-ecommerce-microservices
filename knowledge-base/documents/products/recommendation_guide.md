# Product Recommendations Guide

## How Our Recommendation System Works

Our AI-powered recommendation system analyzes multiple factors to suggest books you'll love:

### Factors We Consider

1. **Purchase History**
   - Books you've bought previously
   - Categories you frequently browse
   - Authors you've purchased from

2. **Browsing Behavior**
   - Products you've viewed
   - Time spent on product pages
   - Items added to wishlist

3. **Customer Segment**
   - VIP customers receive premium recommendations
   - New customers get popular bestsellers
   - Returning customers get personalized picks

4. **Similar Customer Preferences**
   - "Customers who bought this also bought..."
   - Collaborative filtering based on similar profiles

5. **Trending & New Releases**
   - Popular items in your preferred categories
   - New releases from favorite authors

## Types of Recommendations

### Staff Picks

### Bestsellers in Your Categories

## Improving Your Recommendations

### Rate Books
Rate books you've read (1-5 stars) to help us understand your preferences better.

### Complete Your Profile
- Add favorite genres
- List favorite authors
- Indicate reading frequency

### Update Preferences
Go to Account Settings > Reading Preferences to:
- Select preferred categories
- Exclude genres you don't enjoy
- Set language preferences

### Wishlist Books
Adding books to your wishlist signals interest even without purchase.

## Privacy & Recommendations

### Data Used
- Your account activity (purchases, views, ratings)
- Anonymous aggregate patterns from similar readers
- No personal identifying information is shared

### Opting Out
You can disable personalized recommendations:
1. Go to Account Settings
2. Select Privacy
3. Toggle "Personalized Recommendations" off

Note: You'll still see general recommendations based on popularity.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why am I seeing certain recommendations?
Click "Why this recommendation?" on any suggested book to see the reasoning.

### Recommendations don't match my interests
- Update your reading preferences in settings
- Rate some books you've read
- Give the system 2-3 weeks after changes

### I see the same recommendations repeatedly
- Try exploring new categories
- Use filters to discover fresh content
- Check "Hide Already Seen" option

### Can I reset my recommendations?
Yes, go to Account Settings > Privacy > Reset Recommendation Data.
This removes all personalization and starts fresh.
